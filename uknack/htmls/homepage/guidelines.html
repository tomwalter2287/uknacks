A Few Rules to Keep In Mind

Uknack is a driven by a few core values: honesty, trust and good business. We want everyone who uses Uknack to enjoy their experience, and we’ve got a few rules that we hope you’ll keep in mind along the way.

  • We prohibit goods and services that are illegal, federally regulated, illicit, or otherwise dangerous to members of our community.
  • Any good or service claiming to cure, treat, alieve or prevent an illness or medical condition, or purports to do so through any means.
  • Any fraudulent selling tactics, i.e.: exaggerating the qualities of a product or service, selling imitations as the genuine article, or hiding the negative aspects of products or services.
  • Offensive materials (i.e. prejudicial paraphernalia, hate speech, racism, materials encouraging violence against women, others, etc.)
  • Offering alcohol (beers, brews, wines, spirits, etc.,) to members under 21 years of age.
  • Drugs: pills (prescription or designer) narcotics, nicotine, tobacco, hallucinogens, spliffs, vaporizers, or any apparatus that may be used in administering or consuming illegal substances.
  • Selling suggestive services, including: escorting, phone sex, vid-cams, prostitution, porn and other sexual offers.
  • Weapons, replicas of weapons, or any materials that may aid in the construction of weapons and weapon accessories.

If your item or service involves something illegal, regulated, or potentially harmful to members of our community and it is not on this list, please contact us before requesting or posting it on Uknack.
