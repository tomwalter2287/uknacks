A Free Market for Free Spirits

Our Mission

Uknack lets college students turn their talent and skills into their very own businesses. Our mission is to empower college entrepreneurs to engage in free expression, free exchange and free enterprise in a truly free marketplace.

Our Story

Uknack was founded in June 2014 by friends and entrepreneurs in Boston Massachusetts. We wanted to help our friends turn the things they loved to do into fun and fulfilling businesses. In the spirit of entrepreneurship, we designed the first website, hired the founding team, and scraped together funds from their jobs, family and friends to get Uknack up and running.

We built Uknack as a special place to discover student businesses, sell unique items and services, and be inspired by creative student entrepreneurs from all walks of life.

We champion “a free market for free spirits” where students are free to express what they love to do, create sustainable businesses with their talents and connect with like-minded student entrepreneurs from around the college community.

We’re so happy you’re here!

Leading With Our Values

We do what we love, love what we do, and strive to create a place where you can do it too.
We plan, design, and build with you in mind, and always place your needs parallel to our own.
We’re a 100% student founded, student owned and student operated business.
We believe students are the most creative, resourceful and talented entrepreneurs in the world.
Our platform is free for students to use and we allow everyone to keep 100% of what they earn.

Business with Classmates You Trust

In college, we do everything together. We live together, study together, party together, and come together to celebrate each other’s successes. We created Uknack as the perfect place where we can all come together to buy, sell and support each other’s businesses.

We’re so happy to have you here!
